OVERVIEW

Challenge

SJET Student Journey Experience Taskforce is a program initiated by Macquarie University in the year of 2017. The objective of the program is to look at student experiences as a whole. The Starting Episode is one of the major component of SJET program and it focuses on accepting offer & enrolling experiences to identify studentâ€™s pain points and uncover opportunities to improve student's university experiences.

Current state & gap analysis

Through examining current student website, the foundational user research report done previously and communications through other service channels, e.g., comms EDMs & service connect, following gaps have been identified:

  • Step-by-step guides for enrolling & getting students started are missing from current student portal on Macquarie website
  • Multiple webpages about enrolling have duplicated information and are confusing to navigate
  • Information users receive from other channels, e.g., student emails do not align with what's on Macquarie website

Outcome

The exploration of student journey blueprint to map out the key steps & service touch-points and gap analysis between current state (as-is) & future state (to-be) moved the project team to next phase of work.

Two areas in particular were prioritised:

  • Enrolling experience
  • Getting started experience

PHASE 2. UX DESIGN - GETTING STARTED

Getting Started Experience

Challenge

  • A disconnection between current getting started webpage on student portal & the content in EDMs students receive, which end up being problematic & confusing to students.
  • Current getting started information does not act as a step-by-step guide and information does not follow a logical & sequential flow.

Design objectives

  • Create a sequential & time specific "getting started checklist" - a step-by-step guide that is easy to follow
  • Align the content on getting started checklist page with student EDMs contents to avoid confusion
  • Add illustrations indicating the three stages on both webpage & EDMs to enhance the correlation between two communication channels.

PHASE 2. UX DESIGN - ENROLLING

Enrolling Experience

Challenge

The current enrolling experiences Macquarie students have are filled with confusion and headaches. Following are some of the main factors:

  • Compared to the identified future journey map, the current student journey and service touch points do not follow a logical flow, different channels of services are disconnected from one another, e.g. comms communication does't align with the info on university website.
  • A step-by-step guide to facilitate new students with a smooth accepting offer & enrolling journey is missing.
  • Redundant information in university's current student webpage - there are 6 webpages about enrolment and many of them have duplicated information as the webpages are updated by different departments. Users are often led to multiple webpages with very similar information and end up feeling lost.
  • University website for current students lack of information hierarchy & is confusing to navigate

How might we create a single source of truths on current student website, improve functionality of page IA & align information communicated through multiple service channels?

Design objectives

  • Create a repository of enrolment info built in one page
  • Improved Information Architecture of webpage - logical flow of information
  • A step-by-step guide that is easy to navigate and new design components to clearly indicate the steps
